Data Deletion

SplashLens keeps most field data on your device. Here is how to remove it.

Delete Local App Data

Pool profiles, chemistry logs, route notes, checklists, and service report drafts are stored locally on your device. To delete them, clear SplashLens site/app storage in your browser or uninstall the app/PWA and remove its data.

Request Server-Side Deletion

If you sent us an email, joined a waitlist, requested support, or activated an entitlement, email hello@splashlens.com with the subject "SplashLens data deletion request". Include the email address you used so we can locate the record.
