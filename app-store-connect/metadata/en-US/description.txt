SplashLens helps pool service technicians move faster in the field with practical reference tools in one place.

Use SplashLens to look up equipment error codes, run service math, draft visit notes, follow pool-care checklists, and keep common field workflows close at hand. After the first load, core reference tools and notes are designed to stay available for field use.

Optional online scanner tools can help review equipment displays, pool parts, and test-strip images. Scanner results are assistance only: verify results before taking action, follow product labels and manufacturer documentation, and use professional judgment.

SplashLens is not affiliated with or endorsed by pool equipment manufacturers. Brand names may appear only as references for technician lookup and service context.
