# SplashLens IAP Products Draft

Initial App Store submission should be free-core with no IAP products attached.

Use this only as a future planning draft after native Apple In-App Purchase and server-side entitlements are implemented.

## Future Product Ideas

### PartSnap Pro Monthly

- Product ID: `splashlens_partsnap_monthly`
- Type: auto-renewable subscription
- Purpose: higher scanner quota or premium part-identification workflow
- Required before launch: native IAP, receipt validation, entitlement checks, scan metering

### PartSnap Pro Annual

- Product ID: `splashlens_partsnap_annual`
- Type: auto-renewable subscription
- Purpose: annual premium scanner access
- Required before launch: native IAP, receipt validation, entitlement checks, scan metering

### Route Ready Pro Monthly

- Product ID: `splashlens_route_ready_monthly`
- Type: auto-renewable subscription
- Purpose: training modules if Route Ready content and verification are built
- Required before launch: native IAP, module access control, certificate verification, reviewer-visible content

## Do Not Submit Yet

Do not attach these products to the initial app version until:

- Apple IAP is implemented.
- Server-side entitlement checks are implemented.
- Scanner metering is production-ready.
- ASC screenshots and copy match the paid features exactly.
