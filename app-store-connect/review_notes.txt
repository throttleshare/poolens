SplashLens Field Tools is a free iOS wrapper around the SplashLens field PWA:

https://app.splashlens.com/?store=ios

Reviewer path:

1. Launch the app.
2. Use the home screen quick actions.
3. Open error-code lookup or calculator flows.
4. Create or review a service note.
5. Optional: open the scanner flow and select an image/camera input.

No account is required for core use. Core reference tools are intended to work after the first load. Online AI scanner features require internet access and user-selected image input.

Camera/photo access is requested only for user-initiated scanner features. Selected images may be sent to the server-side AI scanner for analysis. Results are assistance only and should be verified by the user.

The initial App Store submission is a free-core build. Direct Stripe checkout and web-subscription CTAs should be hidden in iOS store-wrapper mode. Paid native subscriptions are not part of this submission unless an Apple In-App Purchase implementation is added separately.

SplashLens is not affiliated with or endorsed by pool equipment manufacturers.
