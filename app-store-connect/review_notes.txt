SplashLens is a free utility/reference wrapper around the SplashLens PWA at https://app.splashlens.com/?store=ios.

No account is required for core use. Manual lookup, calculators, notes, filters, and checklists work from the app shell after first load. Camera/photo access is user-initiated and only used for the optional AI scanner. Online AI scan features require internet access.

Store wrapper mode is intended to stay free-core and must not show direct Stripe checkout or web-subscription CTAs. AI scan output is assistance only and should be verified against equipment manuals, calibrated tests, label directions, and professional field judgment.

This build does not include native paid entitlements, unlimited AI scanning, completed training certificates, manufacturer endorsement, or guaranteed diagnosis claims.
