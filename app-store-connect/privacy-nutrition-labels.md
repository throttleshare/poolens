# SplashLens Privacy Nutrition Labels

Initial disclosure posture:

- No account required for core use.
- No tracking.
- No precise location.
- No payment data collected by the initial store wrapper.
- Local service notes may be stored on device/browser storage.
- User-selected images may be uploaded for online AI scanner analysis.
- AI scanner data is for app functionality, not advertising tracking.

Suggested App Store privacy answers for the initial free wrapper:

- Data used to track you: No.
- Data linked to you: None unless crash reporting or account features are added later.
- Data not linked to you: Diagnostics or product interaction only if analytics/crash tooling is confirmed in the native build.
- User content: User-selected photos and text may be processed for app functionality if AI/photo mode is enabled.
- Sensitive info: Do not collect health, financial, or precise-location data.

Do not finalize privacy labels until the Mac build confirms analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, AI endpoint behavior, image retention, account identity, and subscription identity.
