SplashLens Open App

Privacy Policy

SplashLens is an offline-first field reference app for pool service work. This policy explains what data stays on your device and what is used only when you choose online features.

Effective date: May 28, 2026
Short version: most SplashLens field data stays on your device. We do not sell personal data. Camera images are only sent for analysis when you choose to use the online scan feature.

1. Who Operates SplashLens

SplashLens is operated by DeerTech Design LLC. The app is available at https://app.splashlens.com and the marketing site is available at https://splashlens.com.

Contact: hello@splashlens.com.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

SplashLens stores core field information locally in your browser or app webview, including pool profiles, chemistry entries, route notes, checklist progress, and service report drafts.

  • This local field data is not synced to a SplashLens account by default.
  • We cannot recover local field data if you clear browser/app storage or change devices.
  • You can remove local field data by clearing site/app storage from your device.

3. Camera And Scan Features

The app may request camera access so you can scan pool equipment displays, labels, or related field references. Camera access is used only after you grant permission.

If you use the online AI scan feature, the image you submit is sent through our Cloudflare-hosted endpoint to an AI analysis provider for immediate processing. The purpose is to return a field reference result. Do not submit customer private information, faces, payment cards, IDs, or anything unrelated to pool service work.

4. Account, Email, Checkout, And Entitlements

SplashLens can be used without a traditional account. If you join a waitlist, contact us, request access, or activate a paid scan entitlement, we may process the email address or transaction information needed to complete that request.

Payment and checkout flows, when offered, are handled by Stripe or store-provider systems such as Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store full payment card numbers.

5. Analytics And Logs

We may collect limited technical and usage information needed to operate and improve the app, such as page path, feature events, device/browser type, approximate country, timestamps, and error logs. These events are used for product reliability, launch analytics, abuse prevention, and security monitoring.

We do not use advertising IDs, sell personal data, or use third-party ad retargeting inside SplashLens.

6. Third-Party Services

SplashLens may use these service providers to operate the app:

  • Cloudflare for hosting, security, serverless functions, and basic request logs.
  • Anthropic or another AI analysis provider for optional online scan processing.
  • Stripe, Apple, or Google for checkout, subscription, purchase, or store operations if paid features are offered.
  • Email providers for user support, waitlist, or transactional messages.

7. Data Sharing

We share data only as needed to run the app, process user-requested transactions, provide support, comply with law, protect the service, or work with the service providers listed above. We do not sell user data.

8. Children's Privacy

SplashLens is intended for pool service professionals and adults. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Choices And Deletion Requests

You can delete local app data from your device by clearing SplashLens site/app storage. For support records, waitlist records, or other information you sent to us, email hello@splashlens.com and ask for deletion.

If a dedicated deletion page is required for a store review, use https://app.splashlens.com/data-deletion.html.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as SplashLens changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
